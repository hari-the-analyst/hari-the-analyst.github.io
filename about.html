About Me

I’m V. Hariharan, a detail-oriented Data Analyst and SQL Developer with a strong foundation in business intelligence and data visualization. My journey began with a curiosity for the patterns hidden in numbers, which has grown into a drive to bridge the gap between raw data and strategic business decisions.

My work focuses on the entire data lifecycle: from cleaning and analyzing data with SQL and Python to creating compelling, interactive dashboards in Power BI, Tableau, and Excel. I am passionate about crafting visual stories that are not only insightful but also intuitive and easy for all stakeholders to understand.
